    What this service does

    OZONE Shield analyses text messages you paste into the tool for scam indicators using the Claude AI API (Anthropic, PBC). Analysis is performed automatically with no human reviewing your submitted messages. Results are advisory only — they are not a substitute for professional advice and should not be your sole basis for any financial or legal decision.

    Automated decision-making

    All scam verdicts are generated entirely by artificial intelligence. This is disclosed on every result. Under the UK Data (Use and Access) Act 2025 you have the right to query any automated verdict that affects you. Contact us at the address below and we will respond within 30 days.

    Data we collect and why

    • Message content you paste: Transmitted to the Claude AI API (Anthropic, PBC) for analysis. OZONE Shield does not store your message content after the result is returned.
    • Server logs: Netlify (our hosting provider) automatically logs IP addresses and request metadata for security and uptime. Governed by Netlify's Privacy Policy.
    • No tracking: We use no analytics cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party tracking scripts.

    Lawful basis

    Message content is processed under legitimate interests (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) for the purpose of fraud detection and scam prevention — a recognised legitimate interest under the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025. We do not retain your message content after analysis, and the processing directly serves your safety interest.

    Third-party processors

    • Anthropic PBC — Claude AI API, message analysis. US-based; UK data transfers governed by Standard Contractual Clauses. See Anthropic Privacy Policy.
    • Netlify, Inc. — hosting and serverless functions. US-based. See Netlify Privacy Policy.

    Your rights under UK GDPR

    Because OZONE Shield does not store your message content, most data subject rights (access, correction, deletion) apply to Netlify's server logs rather than to OZONE Shield directly. You may contact us with any data query and we will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

    Accuracy & limitations

    AI scam detection is probabilistic. OZONE Shield will sometimes flag legitimate messages (false positives) or miss actual scams (false negatives). Always verify suspicious messages through official channels before taking action. We accept no liability for decisions made based solely on OZONE Shield verdicts.
